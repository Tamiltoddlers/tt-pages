Tamil Numbers

SrideviG Jun 5, 2021 #othertamilprintables

Numbers and Counting is an important lesson to be learnt! Number sense or the basics of learning about numbers, is the first vital math skill a child must develop before reaching kindergarten. But the problem here is they should not learn for the sake of learning! But they should really understand the concept of numbers. How can we do ? It can come only with repeated interactions and by simple methods like counting in front of them. When you offer biscuits to children you can count them as one, two three.. so they really understand what numbers are!  

I have shared a small table and worksheet for numbers. Please check and learn how to pronounce numbers in Tamil. Tamil number counting can also be taught through a famous song! "Ondru yaavarkum thalai ondru...."
